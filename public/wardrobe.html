Automatic background removal

Studio-clean cut-outs. Zero Photoshop skills.

Point, shoot, done. The moment a photo lands, Macgie lifts the garment off its background and drops it into your wardrobe looking like a boutique lookbook — messy bedroom floor and all.

Auto-tagging & smart search

Find "black linen, summer" before you finish typing.

Macgie reads every item — color, fabric feel, category, warmth, formality — and tags it for you. Then search your closet like it's a search engine. The forgotten piece at the back is suddenly one tap away.

  • Tags applied automatically, editable anytime
  • Search by color, category, season or occasion
  • Filters that make a big wardrobe feel small

Wardrobe management

Your wardrobe is a living thing. Star the pieces you reach for, tuck away what's off-season, and get gentle nudges on what you never wear — so decluttering feels clarifying, not guilt-ridden.
