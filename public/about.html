Our story

Built because getting dressed got hard.

Closets keep growing. Confidence doesn't. Macgie started with a simple, stubborn belief — that the clothes you already own are enough, if only something helped you see them.

Every morning, millions of people lose 15 to 30 minutes to the same small crisis: standing in front of a full closet, deciding what to wear. Not because they don't have clothes — but because a wardrobe has no memory. Every day starts from zero.

So we say it anyway, closet bursting: "I have nothing to wear." We buy another black tee to solve a problem more clothes never fix. We wear the same 20% on repeat and forget the rest exists. And underneath it all sits a quiet tax on our confidence — a hundred tiny second-guesses before the day even begins.

Fashion apps are built to help you buy more. Macgie is built to help you use more — and feel good in what you already own.

That distinction is the whole company. The industry's default answer to "what should I wear?" is "buy something new." We think that's the wrong answer to the right question. The better answer is already hanging in your closet — it just needs a brain behind it: something that remembers every piece, understands your taste, reads the weather and the occasion, and hands you an outfit you'll actually feel great in.

That's what we're building. Not another place to catalog clothes, but a wardrobe that thinks with you. A calmer morning. A closet you rediscover. A little more confidence, worn daily.

What we believe

Four principles we don't compromise on.

They shape every screen we ship and every recommendation Macgie makes.

Use more, buy less

The most sustainable, satisfying outfit is one you already own. We optimize for wearing your wardrobe, not expanding it.

Confidence over choice-overload

More options rarely means more certainty. Macgie narrows the field to a look you'll love, and tells you why it works.

Your data is yours

Your wardrobe is personal. We use your data to style you better — never to sell you to advertisers. It stays yours.

Style is personal

There's no single right way to dress. Macgie learns your taste and evolves with it, instead of flattening everyone into a trend.

The founding team

Fashion people and AI people, at one table.

Macgie was started by a small group who kept having the same conversation from opposite ends of it — stylists frustrated that great advice couldn't scale, and engineers convinced that thoughtful AI could carry it. We bonded over a shared irritation: technology had reinvented how we shop, watch and travel, but getting dressed still felt like starting from scratch every single day.

We're deliberately keeping the team small and the mission narrow. No growth-hacking your closet, no nudging you to buy. Just a genuinely useful tool made by people who care about clothes, confidence, and the quiet luxury of a decision made for you.
