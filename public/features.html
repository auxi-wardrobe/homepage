AI Stylist

A stylist that already knows what's in your closet

Macgie reads the weather, your calendar, your mood and your taste — then pulls a look from the clothes you already own. Not a mood board of things to buy. An outfit you can wear in the next five minutes.

  • Context-aware: weather, occasion, mood and what's clean
  • Explains why a look works, so you learn as you go
  • Always styles from what you own — never a shopping list

See the look before you wear it

Stop guessing in front of the mirror. Macgie previews the whole outfit — colors, silhouette, the way it comes together — so you walk out the door sure of yourself instead of second-guessing.

  • Preview colors and silhouette before you get dressed
  • Compare a few options side by side in seconds
  • Build confidence, not another pile on the bed

Smart Digital Wardrobe

Your whole closet, finally organized for you

Every piece you own, in one place — searchable, sortable and auto-tagged by color, category and season. No spreadsheets, no folders. Macgie does the filing so you can find anything in a tap.

  • Automatic tagging by color, category and season
  • Search and filter to find any piece instantly
  • Rediscover the 80% of your closet you forgot you owned

Wardrobe Insights

Understand your closet like never before

See what you actually wear, what's gathering dust, and what each piece really costs per wear. Insights turn a full closet into clear decisions — so your next purchase is the right one, or none at all.

  • Most-worn pieces and true cost-per-wear
  • Spot the gaps and the forgotten favorites
  • Buy smarter, or skip the buy entirely

Travel Mode

Pack a capsule, not your whole closet

Tell Macgie where you're going and for how long. It builds a mix-and-match capsule that fits your bag and covers every day and every occasion — so you wear everything you pack and forget nothing you need.

  • A capsule tailored to your trip length and plans
  • Mix-and-match pieces that stretch into more outfits
  • One carry-on, zero "I have nothing to wear" moments

Smart Import

Add clothes from anywhere in seconds

Snap a photo, drop a screenshot, or paste a product link from any store — Macgie pulls the piece in and removes the background automatically. No editing, no cropping, no Photoshop. Just a clean cutout, ready to style.

  • Import by camera, screenshot or product link
  • Automatic background removal on every upload
  • Wishlist pieces you don't own yet, then style around them

Style Memory

A wardrobe that learns you and evolves

Every look you love, skip or wear teaches Macgie something. It remembers your favorites, notices your seasonal shifts, and follows your taste as it changes — so the advice gets more you with every passing week.

  • Learns from what you wear, love and ignore
  • Adapts to seasons and your evolving style
  • Gets sharper and more personal every day
