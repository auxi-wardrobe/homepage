About Macgie

Boilerplate

Short

Macgie is the AI wardrobe and styling app that gives your closet a brain. It recommends outfits from clothes you already own, lets you see the look before you wear it, and learns your taste over time — so getting dressed takes seconds, not stress.

Long

Macgie is an AI-powered personal wardrobe and styling app for iOS. Instead of asking people to buy more, Macgie helps them get more out of what they already own. Users build a digital wardrobe by photo, screenshot or link — Macgie removes backgrounds and tags each piece automatically — then receive outfit recommendations tailored to their taste, body, the weather, the occasion and their mood. A visualization feature lets people preview a full look before they get dressed, and the app grows smarter with every outfit loved or skipped.

Founded on the belief that a full closet should never feel empty, Macgie positions itself as an operating system for the wardrobe: less decision fatigue, less waste, and more confidence in what you already have. The app is currently in early access via TestFlight, with a free tier and an optional subscription. Macgie is a remote-first company.

The story

Founder story

Macgie began with a familiar contradiction: a full closet and the feeling of having nothing to wear. The founding team kept noticing that the problem was rarely a lack of clothes — it was a lack of memory. Wardrobes hold years of good decisions, but every morning starts from zero.

So they set out to build the missing layer: software that remembers what you own, understands how the pieces work together, and helps you decide. What started as a simple styling experiment grew into a broader ambition — to help millions of people love, and actually wear, what they already have. That mission still guides every release.
