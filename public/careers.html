Why Macgie

A rare mix: real purpose, real ownership.

We're early, we're small, and every person here shapes what Macgie becomes.

A mission that matters

Less waste, less overwhelm, more confidence. We help people feel good in what they own — and buy with intention, not anxiety.

Remote-first, always

Work from wherever you do your best thinking. We're async by default, deliberate about meetings, and we trust you with your calendar.

Early-stage impact

Join before the product is finished and your fingerprints are everywhere — the roadmap, the craft, the culture. This is ground-floor work.

Benefits that respect you

Meaningful equity, competitive pay, real time off, hardware you choose, and a stipend for the desk that keeps you comfortable.

Craft over headcount

We'd rather ship something considered than something big. Small team, high standards, and room to do the best work of your career.

People who care

Kind, direct, low-ego teammates who love the details. We give real feedback, celebrate the wins, and take the mission seriously — not ourselves.
