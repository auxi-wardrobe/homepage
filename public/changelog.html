Changelog

What's new in Macgie

We ship often and sweat the details. Here's what's changed lately — newest first. Want the latest builds first? Join the beta.

Version 1.6 · June 2026

Travel mode & sharper visualization

  • Travel mode. Tell Macgie where you're going and for how long — it builds a mix-and-match capsule that fits your bag.
  • Higher-fidelity visualization. Outfit previews now render with more accurate fabric texture, drape and color.
  • Smoother onboarding with the sample wardrobe, plus a handful of stability fixes.

Version 1.5 · May 2026

Faster imports, everywhere

  • 2× faster imports. Adding clothes by photo or link is now noticeably quicker, with better auto-detection of the garment.
  • Improved background removal. Cleaner cutouts around hair, straps and complex edges.
  • Bulk import: add several screenshots at once and Macgie sorts them for you.

Version 1.4 · April 2026

Wardrobe insights

  • Insights dashboard. See most-worn pieces, cost-per-wear, wardrobe gaps and forgotten items at a glance.
  • Outfit calendar. Plan looks for the week and log what you actually wore.
  • Refined recommendation ranking so favorites surface sooner.

Version 1.3 · March 2026

Smarter, more personal styling

  • Style memory. Macgie now remembers what you love and skip, and explains why a look works.
  • Weather-aware picks. Recommendations now factor in the day's forecast automatically.
  • Faster app launch and a lighter, calmer home screen.

Version 1.2 · February 2026

The first public beta

  • Sample wardrobe. Start getting recommendations in the first minute, no uploads required.
  • Outfit canvas. Mix, match and save unlimited outfit combinations.
  • Macgie opens to the world on TestFlight. Thank you to our earliest testers.
