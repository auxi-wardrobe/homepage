FAQ

Questions, answered.

Everything worth asking about Macgie — how the styling works, what happens to your data, which platforms we're on, and what it actually costs. Honest, no fine-print games.

Getting started

The basics.

What is Macgie, exactly?

Macgie is an AI stylist and digital wardrobe in one. It recommends outfits from clothes you already own, lets you visualize looks before you wear them, and quietly learns your taste. Think of it less as a closet organizer and more as an operating system for your wardrobe.

Is this just another closet inventory app?

No. Cataloging your clothes is the starting point, not the destination. Plenty of apps stop at a tidy grid of your things. Macgie's job is to decide with you — recommending outfits, visualizing them, and explaining why a look works.

Do I have to upload my whole wardrobe before it's useful?

Never. Macgie ships with a ready-made sample wardrobe, so you get real recommendations in your first minute — no empty screens. When you're ready, replace it with your own clothes at whatever pace suits you.

What's the sample wardrobe for?

It exists so you can feel the magic immediately instead of doing homework first. Explore recommendations and visualizations right away, then swap in your own pieces one photo at a time. Most wardrobe apps make you upload everything before anything happens; we flipped that.

How long until I get my first outfit?

Under a minute. Open the app, and Macgie is already styling looks from the sample wardrobe. Adding your own clothes takes a little longer, but you never start from a blank screen.

Do I need to be good at fashion to use it?

Not at all — that's rather the point. Macgie does the styling thinking for you and explains its choices, so you learn as you go. If getting dressed stresses you out, this is built for you.

Styling & AI

How the recommendations work.

How does Macgie know what suits me?

It considers your style preferences, body profile, the weather where you are, the occasion, your mood, what's actually clean and available, and your wear history. Then it learns from every look you save, wear or skip — so the picks sharpen over time.

Can I really see myself in an outfit?

Yes. Macgie visualizes the full look so you can preview colors, silhouette and styling before you get dressed. It turns "I think this might work" into "I can see that it does."

Does it explain why a look works?

It does. Rather than handing you an outfit and expecting blind trust, Macgie tells you the reasoning — the color balance, the proportions, the occasion fit. Over time you're not just following orders, you're learning to style yourself.

Will it tell me to go buy new things?

No. Macgie is built to help you use what you already own, not to send you shopping. The entire premise is that you don't need more clothes — you need to see the ones you have. No affiliate nudges, no "complete the look, buy now."

Can Macgie help with a capsule or travel wardrobe?

Yes. Tell it where you're going and for how long, and travel mode builds a mix-and-match capsule that fits your bag and your itinerary. It's the same thinking behind capsule wardrobes — fewer pieces, more outfits.

Does it actually help with decision fatigue and ADHD?

That's one of the most common reasons people love it. When choosing is the hard part, Macgie removes the paralysis by narrowing thousands of possibilities down to a few great looks. Several beta testers with ADHD describe it as the calmest their closet has ever felt.

How accurate are the recommendations?

Good on day one and better every week, because Macgie learns your taste as you use it. Early on it leans on your preferences and the sample data; the more you wear, save and skip, the more personal it gets. It's a stylist that studies you, not a random shuffle.

Wardrobe & imports

Getting your clothes in.

How do I add my own clothes?

Four ways: snap a photo with your camera, drop in a screenshot, paste a website URL, or share a product link from wherever you shop. Macgie handles the rest — background removal and tagging happen automatically.

Does background removal really work automatically?

Yes. Upload a photo and Macgie cuts out the garment for you — no Photoshop, no manual masking, no editing skills. You just point and shoot.

Can I import from online stores and screenshots?

Absolutely. Paste a product link or a screenshot from a shopping app, and Macgie pulls the item into your wardrobe. It's the fastest way to add pieces you bought online or spotted while browsing.

Does Macgie organize everything for me?

It auto-tags each piece by color, category and season, so your wardrobe becomes searchable without you filling in forms. You can always adjust tags, but the goal is that you rarely need to.

Is there a limit to how many items I can add?

Your digital wardrobe is available on every plan, including Free. The paid tiers focus on unlimited AI recommendations and visualizations rather than capping how many clothes you own — because a wardrobe you can't fully add isn't much of a wardrobe.

Does Macgie work offline?

You can browse your wardrobe and saved outfits offline, but AI recommendations, visualizations and imports need a connection since that's where the styling brain lives. We're always working to make more of it available on the go.

Privacy & data

Your closet, your business.

Is my wardrobe data private?

Yes. Your wardrobe is yours. We use your data to personalize your experience and improve your recommendations — never to sell you to advertisers. The full details live in our Privacy Policy.

Do you sell my data?

No. We don't sell your personal data or your wardrobe to third parties, and we don't run an ad network off your closet. Our business is a subscription, not your information.

Are my photos used to train models?

We're careful and transparent here. Your photos power your experience first. Any use to improve Macgie is handled in line with our Privacy Policy, and we're committed to giving you clear controls over your data.

Can I delete my data?

Yes. You can delete individual items or your whole account, and when you delete your account we remove your personal wardrobe data. It's your closet — you get to close the door on it whenever you want.

Can I share outfits with friends?

Sharing a look you love is part of the fun, so you can export and send outfits. What you don't have to worry about is anything private leaving your wardrobe without you choosing to share it.

Billing & account

Plans, platforms and payments.

What does Macgie cost?

There's a Free plan you can keep forever, Pro at $7.99/mo for unlimited styling, and Pro+ at $14.99/mo for premium models and concierge styling. During early access, everything is free. See the full breakdown on the pricing page.

Is the Free plan free forever?

Yes, genuinely. Free includes your digital wardrobe, the sample wardrobe, background removal and a daily allowance of AI recommendations and visualizations. It's a real product, not a trial that expires.

Can I cancel anytime?

Any time, in a couple of taps through your App Store subscription settings — no email, no phone call. You keep your paid features until the period you've paid for ends, then roll back to Free with nothing deleted.

Which platforms is Macgie on?

Macgie is on iOS today via the TestFlight beta. Android is on the roadmap. Join the beta and we'll let you know the moment an Android build is ready.

What languages does Macgie support?

Macgie is available in English today, and we're expanding to more languages as we grow. If your language matters to you, tell us in the beta — it helps us prioritize.
