Questions

Good questions, honest answers.

Is Macgie just another closet inventory app?

No. Cataloging is the starting point, not the product. Macgie's job is to decide with you — recommending outfits from what you own, visualizing them, and explaining why they work.

Do I have to upload my whole wardrobe first?

Never. You start with a ready-made sample wardrobe and get recommendations in the first minute. Swap in your own clothes whenever you like, at your own pace.

How does Macgie know what suits me?

It considers your style preferences, body profile, the weather, the occasion, your mood, what's actually clean and available, and your wear history — then learns from every look you love or skip.

Can I really see myself in an outfit?

Yes. Macgie visualizes the look so you can preview colors, silhouette and styling before you get dressed — building confidence, not guesswork.

How do I add my clothes?

Camera, screenshot, website or product link. Background removal is automatic — no editing skills required.

Is my wardrobe data private?

Your wardrobe is yours. We use your data to personalize your experience, never to sell you to advertisers. See our Privacy Policy.
