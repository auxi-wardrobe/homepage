Pricing questions

The money stuff, plainly.

How does billing work?

Pro and Pro+ are billed monthly or annually through your Apple App Store account. Annual billing saves you roughly two months versus paying month to month. You'll always see the price before you confirm, and Apple handles the charge securely.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes — cancel with a couple of taps in your App Store subscription settings, no email or phone call required. You keep Pro features until the end of the period you've already paid for, then roll back to Free. Nothing is deleted.

Is the Free plan actually free forever?

Genuinely, yes. Free isn't a countdown to a paywall — it includes your digital wardrobe, the sample wardrobe, background removal and a daily allowance of AI recommendations and visualizations. Stay on it as long as you like.

Is there a student discount?

We're building one. Students are exactly the people juggling a tight budget and a busy schedule, so a discounted Pro is on our roadmap — join the beta and we'll let you know the moment it's live.

What's your refund policy?

Refunds for App Store subscriptions are handled by Apple. If something went wrong, request a refund through Apple's Report a Problem page — and email us too, because we'd rather fix the issue than see you go.

Does the Free plan really do anything useful?

It does. You can build your real wardrobe, get genuine AI outfit recommendations, visualize looks and import clothes with automatic background removal. Free is a real product — Pro simply removes the limits and adds travel mode, insights and the calendar.
