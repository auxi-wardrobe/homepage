Pricing questions, answered.

Will Macgie help me shop less?

That is the goal. Wardrobe insights show you what you already own and love, help you avoid buying duplicates, and reduce impulse purchases so you shop less but dress better.

What is Macgie, exactly?

Macgie is a digital wardrobe and AI stylist. It organizes the clothes you already own, then recommends and visualizes outfits for your day — so getting dressed takes seconds, not twenty minutes.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your wardrobe and style model are yours. We don't sell your data, and everything lives behind your account with privacy built in from the start.

Do I need to be good at fashion to use it?

Not at all — that's the point. Macgie does the styling. You rate a few looks and it learns your taste, so the recommendations feel like you without the effort.

Do I need a huge wardrobe for it to be useful?

No. Start with our ready-made sample wardrobe and add your own pieces at your own pace. Even a small closet unlocks more combinations than you'd expect.
