Triangle Body Shape — Complete Style Guide

Elise TranJune 18, 20266 min read
Back to Journal

Triangle body shape — also known as the "pear" shape — is one of the most common body types out there. The hips are the widest point, the shoulders are narrower, and the waist is well defined.

The goal isn't to hide anything. It's balance: draw a little attention up top, keep the line through the hip clean, and let your waist do the work it already does well.

Macgie learns your proportions over time and factors them into every recommendation, so the outfit you're handed already accounts for your shape — no styling rules to memorize.
